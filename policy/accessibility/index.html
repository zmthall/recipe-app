Accessibility Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2025

MenuMate is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all people, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to meet or exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA wherever possible and continuously work to improve user experience for everyone.

Our Accessibility Practices

  • Using semantic HTML to support screen readers
  • Ensuring color contrast meets accessibility standards
  • Providing keyboard navigability throughout the site
  • Supporting scalable text and responsive layout
  • Testing pages with accessibility tools and manual audits

Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing commitment. We are continually working to enhance our site's usability and accessibility features based on user feedback, evolving technology, and updated guidelines.

Feedback & Assistance

If you encounter any accessibility barriers while using MenuMate, or if you have suggestions on how we can improve your experience, please fill out the form below or email us directly at zack@yourmenumate.com.
