About MenuMate

Built by a developer who loves food — designed to simplify meal planning, boost productivity, and help you live better.

Hi, I'm a full-stack developer with a deep passion for food, health, and creating tools that make life easier. MenuMate started as a personal project — a recipe management and meal planning app that I could rely on daily. I built this tool to take control of my nutrition, organize my cooking, and make everyday food decisions less stressful.

Packed with features like a recipe importer, macro tracker, food journal, and grocery list generator, MenuMate became the backbone of my routine. It helped me get back into shape, streamline my eating habits, and bring more order to my day-to-day.

This isn't just an app I made — it's one I use every single day. I utilize every feature:

  • Saving and editing my favorite recipes
  • Planning weekly meals to reduce decision fatigue
  • Creating organized grocery carts that save time and cut waste
  • Tracking macros and journaling food for personal fitness goals
  • Reusing saved meal plans for efficient prep and consistency

I created MenuMate to serve my life — and I know it can do the same for yours.
